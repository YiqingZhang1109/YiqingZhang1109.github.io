Development Department

Our job is to empower the Project Nous and maintain organizational stickiness through alumni networks, online activities, and external cooperation. Here, you can exercise all kinds of abilities: from outreach to event operations, from strategic planning to brand building.

Academic Department

The Academic Department is mainly responsible for controlling the academic level and course quality of the planned reading camp. It also organizes the 2020-2021 Winter Book Club together, along with the Operation Department.


Advertising Department

Our job is mainly based on WeChat public accounts. It is committed to publishing information on reading camps and other activities, recruitment information, displaying the style of the Gezhi program, and sharing good books and opinions. We need you who are passionate about communication, writing and various media to join us!

IT Department

Our job is to provide digital support for all activities of the Gezhi Project, including but not limited to the preparation of the Gezhi Project website and mini programs, the design of a reader recruitment system and an online alumni network platform, etc.
