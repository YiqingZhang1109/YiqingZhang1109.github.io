ABOUT US

Who We Are

Bring liberal arts education and small class teaching to students in remote areas.

Project Nous was founded in 2016. It is a non-profit education project organized by a group of Chinese college students from all over the world. It aims to bring small class seminars and reading experience to high school students in non-first-tier cities and counties. Since its establishment, we have recruited a group of excellent readers every summer and winter, and help them form a habit of dialectical thinking and establish a connection between themselves and the world by leading students to read classics and class discussions. We guide students to read the classic texts of the East and the West in depth, analyze and deconstruct these texts in the form of a discussion class, and give students an opportunity to present, communicate and freely debate. Through the study and communication during the study camp, students can not only enrich theoretical knowledge, but also have a clearer understanding of themselves, re-examine their values ​​and outlook on life, and make dialectical thinking a habit.

What We Do

Our camps in different places!

In the past few years, we have carried out summer liberal arts education camps in middle schools in Pujiang County, Yuhuan County, Zhejiang Province, Zhongfang County, Hunan Province, Yulin City, Shaanxi Province and other places, and have achieved great results. This year's epidemic has brought many unprecedented challenges to the Gezhi Project. However, with the support of all parties, we still brought the summer study camp to the four cities of Changsha in Hunan, Guiyang and Kaili in Guizhou, and Yantai in Shandong. The scale reached the largest since its establishment and received positive responses from the participants.
