We Are Waiting for You

In the past few years, we have carried out summer liberal arts education camps in middle schools in Pujiang County, Yuhuan County, Zhejiang Province, Zhongfang County, Hunan Province, Yulin City, Shaanxi Province and other places, and have achieved great results. This year's epidemic has brought many unprecedented challenges to the Project Nous. However, with the support of all parties, we still brought the summer study camp to the four cities of Changsha in Hunan, Guiyang and Kaili in Guizhou, and Yantai in Shandong. The scale reached the largest since its establishment and received positive responses from the participants.


The hard work of the readers and the enthusiastic participation of the students are the inexhaustible motivation for Projecy Nous to continue to move forward. In the coming year, we hope to bring the reading camp to more high school students, give them a non-traditional and discussion-style learning experience, and encourage more open and inclusive exchange of ideas. Therefore, the Project Nous team is now open for recruitment. No matter whether you have been a member of Project Nous, as long as you agree with our ideals and are passionate about education, you are welcome to sign up to join us and bring fresh blood to Gezhi!
